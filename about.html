About

I'm someone who loves to be creative. It is a way of breathing. Wether it's music, dancing, theatre, writing or drawing. Every way I can use to get my feelings, others feelings and stories out there. As a songwriter I love getting deep and it is a way for me to express someone elses life or my own. It doesnt matter if it's 10 people that listens or 2 million. If I can get someone to feel like they connect or "someone understands me". Then it is worth it. We sing what can't be spoken and we dance what can't be sung. Im a quirky, funny, loving and fierce woman who wants to tell a story. Do you want to be on my journey?
